ABOUT PAK COFFEE SHOP

OUR STORY
Founded in 1978, PAK Coffee Shop has been a cornerstone of the Nairobi community. Our passion for high-quality coffee and sustainable practices drives us to serve the best brews in town. From ethically sourced beans to freshly baked pastries, everything we offer is crafted with love.
